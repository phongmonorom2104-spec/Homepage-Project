Admissions Process

1. Apply Online

Complete the online application form with your personal and academic details. Make sure all information is accurate.

2. Submit Documents

Upload transcripts, certificates, and identification documents. International students may need additional documents.

3. Interview & Evaluation

Attend an interview or assessment if required. Our admissions team evaluates all applications carefully.

4. Admission Result

Receive your admission decision via email. Accepted students will get instructions for enrollment and orientation.

Charity for the Year​

The leadership of Western University and Western International School has allocated funds from charitable donations received from various philanthropists to support soldiers and displaced civilians affected by the incursions along the Cambodia-Thailand border. This humanitarian assistance was provided through: The Ministry of Education, Youth, and Sports, with a total fund of 40,000,000 riels (forty million riels). The Association of Higher Education Institutions, with a total fund of 12,000,000 riels (twelve million riels). This effort reflects the long-standing spirit of "One Nation, One Voice, One Heart," which represents a shared responsibility among national leadership, the private sector, and citizens to support and unite in standing together during this severe national crisis.
